Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SellStream

SellStream is a live marketplace where Pokemon and Sports card collectors can buy, sell, and break packs in real-time via live streaming. Think Whatnot meets eBay, built specifically for card collectors.
SellStream offers three tiers: Free (8% transaction fee), Pro at $29/month (5% fee), and Premium at $99/month (3% fee). Payment processing is 2.9% across all tiers (passed through at cost via Stripe).
Pokemon TCG, Sports cards (baseball, basketball, football, hockey), Yu-Gi-Oh!, Magic: The Gathering, and other trading card games. Both graded (PSA, BGS, CGC) and raw cards are welcome.
Sellers list pack break events. Buyers purchase spots before the break starts. The seller goes live, opens packs on camera, and cards are assigned to spot holders in real-time. Cards ship directly to buyers.
We integrate with PSA (Professional Sports Authenticator), BGS (Beckett Grading Services), and CGC (Certified Guaranty Company) for verified grading data on listings.
Payments are processed via Stripe Connect. Sellers receive funds directly to their bank account within 2-3 business days after a completed sale. Crypto payouts are also planned.
